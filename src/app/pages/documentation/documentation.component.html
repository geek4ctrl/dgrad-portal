General information

INTEGRATED LOGIRAD SERVICES

LOGIRAD centralizes the main modules described in the user manual: subjects, declarations, files, taxation, authorizations, control, recovery and litigation.

Subjects

  • Registry of natural persons, legal entities and NGOs
  • Addresses and identifiers for declarations
  • Activation and deactivation of subjects
  • Tax identification number (NIF)

Declarations

  • Links subjects to periodic taxes
  • Records assessments (simple or multiple)
  • Identifies defaulters and potential defaulters
  • Budget articles and periodicity

Files

  • Tracks requests and supporting documents
  • Reception and acknowledgement of receipt
  • Multi-attachment support per file
  • Initiate taxation directly from a file

Taxation

  • Standard, ex-officio and regularisation notes
  • Taxation based on assessments
  • Attachments and document validation
  • Chef-Taxateur validation workflow

Authorizations

  • Payment notices and pay orders
  • Special and ex-officio authorisations
  • Rejected and cancelled notice registers
  • Conformity review by ordering officer

Control

  • Compliance of authorisations and taxations
  • Targeted control missions
  • Access to taxation and payment registers
  • Controller conformity reports

Recovery

  • Uptake of authorised revenues
  • Clearance and discharge certificates
  • Rolls, final notices and enforcement
  • Outstanding balance monitoring

Litigation

  • Administrative and judicial appeals
  • Effects of complaints on taxations
  • Decisions on notices and amounts due
  • Dispute tracking and resolution
